About Us

Big Movies is one of the best & Nepal’s First Multiplex operated by Vishal Group.

We have 3 Screens with a total seating capacity of 887 seats, providing world class auditoriums. We are enabled by BARCO 2K Digital Projection System, Crystal Clear DTS Sound System along with DOLBY ATMOS as an upgrade. We currently have one screen with high quality 3D effect serving best sound and picture.

Our Mission: “To Offer a world class cinematic experience.”

We strongly believe in bringing the highest exhibition standards to make your movie experience, “entertaining and memorable” with our services, starting from Ticket counter to EXIT.

We look forward to keep on growing with innovative ideas in respect of latest technologies, advance operating systems, best offers and future expansion to provide complete entertainment experience for our patrons.
